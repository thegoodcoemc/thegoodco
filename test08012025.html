Parenting Talks - Nurturing Kindness to animals at home

What will be covered:

1. Simple yet meaningful ways to foster compassion and responsibility for animals.
2. Fun, hands-on ideas for building bonds with pets or connecting with nature.
3. TGES resources to support you

Who should attend?

Parents eager to teach their children how to care for animals and respect all living beings.

Meet The Speakers

Dr Cynthia Wong

About the speaker

Dr Cynthia Wong is a seasoned marine ecologist with 10 years of experience in the academic and research industry.

She is committed to empowering young individuals with a deep understanding and profound appreciation for marine ecology.

Her dedication has motivated her to educate young minds at different Institutes of Higher Education. Through her teaching, she not only imparts knowledge but also cultivates close collaboration with academia and conservation partners, enhancing her students' learning journey in science-related fields.



Coach Aifa ahmad

About the speaker

Aifa Ahmad is a seasoned trainer and coach with over 20 years of experience in teambuilding, facilitation, and outdoor education.

Her passion for training began since she was a teen, where she discovered the power of sharing knowledge and inspiring others through her work with outdoor adventure training. Over the years, Aifa has cultivated expertise in wellness and entrepreneurship, delivering impactful programs that empower individuals and foster personal growth.

Her work has reached diverse audiences, from students to professionals, as she continues to champion meaningful connections and positive change through her dynamic and heartfelt facilitation.



Jeryl Yap

About the speaker

Jeryl Yep is an innovator and trained chemical engineer with a wealth of experience in environmental leadership.

His career journey began with roles that focused on driving environmental responsibility, including his time as an environmental engineer at a local NGO, where he led certifications, product development, and training programs. He also championed energy-saving initiatives and guided students and industries in adopting more sustainable practices.

Jeryl continues to inspire collaboration and create scalable solutions that empower individuals and businesses to make a lasting impact on the environment.
