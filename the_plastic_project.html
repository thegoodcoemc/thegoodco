Lets Talk About Plastics

Plastic seamlessly integrates into our daily lives, and with it comes issues like the choking of marine wildlife, soil damage, and human health risks.

Even after we throw it away, plastics stick around for a long time. Plastic is tough and it doesn't break down easily, which adds to pollution in the environment.

This is on top of the production phase of plastic involving the extraction of raw materials and consumption of an immense amount of energy.

Why are we dependent on plastics?
