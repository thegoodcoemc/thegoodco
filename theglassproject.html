Educational institution visits

Our Objectives

To inculcate a love for animals in our youths (11-16Y), equipping them with knowledge on animal welfare.

  • Increase interest in animal welfare and develop empathy for animals at a young age
  • Acquisition of specific skill sets in pet handling and care
  • Gain knowledge of how they can make a difference in animal welfare

    • During the workshop, participants will learn:

    1. Basic pet care
    2. Knowledge on animal welfare
    3. Creation of animal enrichment toys

    We aim to inculcate empathy among students and equip them with the knowledge on animal welfare so that they can spearhead their own animal welfare projects such as fundraising for shelters/rescues and educating the public what they have learnt on animal welfare.

    Contact us at hello@animalwelfareacademy.com for more information.


    Additional points to note

    Sometimes, AWA brings live animals onsite during animal interaction sessions. Animals are sentient individuals with their own personalities- with likes, dislikes, and fears. AWA reserves the right to remove any animal from the premises should the animal be feeling (but not limited to): stressed, angry, and/or scared. This protects both the participants and the animals so we can ensure a safe environment for both parties.
